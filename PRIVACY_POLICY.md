# Privacy Policy for AstroSeeker

**Last updated: March 9, 2026**

## Overview

AstroSeeker is a natal chart calculation app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

AstroSeeker does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All natal chart calculations are performed locally on your device.

### Location Data

- The app requests access to your device's location to determine coordinates for chart calculations.
- Location data is used **only on your device** and is never transmitted to any server.
- Location permission is optional. You can manually select a location on the map instead.

### Data Stored on Device

- Birth date, time, and location preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences.
- This data never leaves your device.

### Third-Party Services

- **OpenStreetMap**: The app loads map tiles from OpenStreetMap servers. OpenStreetMap may log standard web request data (IP address, request time). See [OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy).
- **Google Generative AI**: When you use the AI insights feature, your chart data is sent to Google's API for processing. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Analytics

AstroSeeker does **not** use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

AstroSeeker does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page.
